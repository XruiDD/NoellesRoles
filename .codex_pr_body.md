- add the new `Riot Patrol` role kit with `Riot Shield` and `Riot Fork`
- make custom shield/trident-style items render through vanilla special item renderer paths

## Details

### Riot Patrol

- `Riot Shield`
  - uses shield-style rendering
  - blocks frontal melee while raised
  - locks camera turning by canceling mouse-look sensor updates
  - left click shoves nearby players with shorter reach
  - applying a shove now also starts the 30s cooldown and blocks further left click use during cooldown

- `Riot Fork`
  - uses trident-style rendering
  - roots both players for 8 seconds
  - rooting now links both participants together
  - if the player who applied the fork stops holding the fork before the effect ends, both rooted states are cleared immediately

### Cleanup

- remove the temporary singleplayer testing UI and networking path
- keep the rest of the Riot Patrol work intact
